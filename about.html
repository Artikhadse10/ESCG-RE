About Us

ESCG is a web application which is designed to take tests of the students who have been deprived from an opportunity to test their skills due to this collapsed education system due to pandemic condition. This app is a basic demonstration of the system that how we can conduct the exam and also generate so authentication about present to the exam.

This demo application consists of tests on Artificial Intelligence, Python and Web Development. Also a Resume generator can be seen on the webpage. On clicking on the button we will have to fill some basic details of the student and the resume will be generated automatically.

This system will is made in consideration of the pandemic condition but it can also make the life of the students life easy and helpful also for the college and universities to take tests with ease. This test system will make a great transformation if we will be able to make this system as a part of the life. As the student will be able to give the exam from any place in the world. The students will be free to take the exam according to the time mentioned by the college but also can have it again if any problem occurs.
